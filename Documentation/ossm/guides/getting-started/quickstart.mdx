## Choose Your Path

You have three options for getting an OSSM, depending on your experience level and how hands-on you want to be.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Buy Assembled" icon="box" href="/ossm/guides/getting-started/official-add-ons">
    Purchase a fully assembled OSSM from R+D Labs. Ready to use out of the box with no building required.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Buy a DIY Kit" icon="screwdriver-wrench" href="/ossm/guides/getting-started/build-guide/step-0.0_introduction">
    Get a complete DIY kit from R+D Labs with all components included. Follow the build guide to assemble your own OSSM.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Source Your Own Parts" icon="print" href="/ossm/guides/getting-started/build-guide/step-1.0_required_tools_and_parts">
    Source components from various suppliers and print your own 3D parts. Best for experienced makers who want full control.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
