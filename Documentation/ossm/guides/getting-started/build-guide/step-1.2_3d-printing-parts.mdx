Before you begin assembly, you need to print all required parts. This page lists every component you need to print for a complete OSSM build.

<Note>
This guide covers the official OSSM build. You can find community modifications in the [OSSM Mods](/Hardware/ossm-mods) section to customize your build.
</Note>

## Print settings

<Tip>
For best results, use PETG or ABS filament with at least 4 perimeters and 40% infill. PLA works but may deform under sustained use due to heat buildup.
</Tip>
