## Purchased Parts

You need to purchase the following components to build your OSSM. This list covers the official build configuration.

<Note>
This guide covers the complete official OSSM build. Many modifications are available to customize your specific build—check the [community mods](/ossm/guides/community) section for alternatives.
</Note>

### Motor

<Frame caption="NEMA 23 Servo Motor – IHSV57-30-18">
  <img src="/images/_images/legacy_instructions/legacy_assembly_instructions_IHS57-30-18.jpg" alt="IHSV57-30-18 servo motor with cable" />
</Frame>

**NEMA 23 Servo Motor – IHSV57-30-18**

Most builders choose the 180W model for maximum power. The 140W and 100W variants are also available if you prefer lower power consumption.

<Warning>
Avoid the StepperOnline version of this motor due to compatibility issues with the OSSM firmware.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Purchase belt in bulk. While 500mm is the minimum for a standard build, having extra belt allows for adjustments and future modifications.
</Tip>

### Hardware

<Frame caption="Metric cap screw assortment kit">
  <img src="/images/_images/legacy_instructions/legacy_assembly_instructions_metric_cap_screw_kit.png" alt="Assorted metric cap head screws in organizer case" />
</Frame>

**Metric Cap Screw Kit** (approximately $20 USD)

The build uses M3, M4, and M5 screws in various lengths:

- Most M5 cap head screws are 20mm or 25mm long
- The middle pivot mount requires 4× M5×35mm screws
