## Our story

Kinky Makers was founded by Alex and James in Toronto, Canada in 2020. It began as a physical space for creators to gather and build devilish creations together.

More importantly, it gave early members a space to normalize their kinks through a shared interest in engineering and hacking. Taboo questions like "how many watts does a stepper motor need for anal penetration?" became commonplace, offering a space to educate, discuss, and feel safe.
