The OSSM roadmap reflects our collaborative development process. Research and Desire works alongside community members to set priorities and update the roadmap quarterly.

## Contribute to the roadmap

Want to help shape the future of OSSM? You can influence the roadmap in several ways:

- **Suggest features** — Open a GitHub issue describing your idea and its benefits
- **Vote on priorities** — React to existing issues to show community interest
- **Join quarterly planning** — Reach out to core leadership to participate in roadmap discussions

<Tip>
Looking to start contributing? Issues labeled **good first issue** are specifically selected for new contributors. These provide a great entry point into the codebase.
</Tip>

## Get involved

If you'd like to participate in roadmap planning or have ideas for the project's direction, reach out to the core leadership team through GitHub or the community Discord.
