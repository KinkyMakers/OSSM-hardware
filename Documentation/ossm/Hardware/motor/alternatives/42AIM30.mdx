The 42AIM30 is a variant of the Gold Motor with identical internals and specifications in a more compact cylindrical form factor.

<Warning>
The 42AIM30 has limited mounting support and requires custom hardware. Only consider this motor if you have specific size constraints.
</Warning>

## Overview

The 42AIM30 (sometimes called the "Round Motor") shares the same internal components as the standard 57AIM30 Gold Motor but removes the heatsink housing, leaving only the cylindrical motor body with connectors at the base.

## Mounting Considerations

Unlike the 57AIM30 with its bolt-through mounting ears, you must mount the 42AIM30 using machine screws threaded directly into the motor face:

- Measure the exact screw length required for your mounting solution
- Use the correct thread pitch for the motor face holes
- Check Discord community resources for tested mounting solutions

<Tip>
Join the KinkyMakers Discord to find community-designed housings compatible with the 42AIM30.
</Tip>

## Thermal Performance

The 42AIM30 lacks the integrated heatsink found on the 57AIM30. When enclosed in a 3D-printed housing:

- Monitor motor temperature during extended use
- Consider adding ventilation holes to your housing design
- Reduce duty cycle if you observe thermal throttling

Community testing on thermal performance is ongoing. Share your temperature data on Discord to help improve recommendations.
</Info>
