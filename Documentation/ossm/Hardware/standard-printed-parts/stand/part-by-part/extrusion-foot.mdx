## Function

Protective foot for the floor-contact ends of the 3030 extrusion stand. This component:

- Press-fits into the open ends of 3030 extrusion legs
- Provides a flat, wide contact patch for stability
- Accepts adhesive non-slip furniture pads (1.5"/38 mm square) on the bottom surface

<Tip>
Material: PETG or PLA+ recommended. TPU 95A is an option for maximum grip, but expect a tighter press-fit — test-fit before printing all four.
</Tip>
