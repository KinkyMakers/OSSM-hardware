## Function

One of two matching plates that create the adjustable pivot on the OSSM stand. This component:

- Mounts to 3030 aluminum extrusion via M6 T-slot nuts
- Provides the left-side pivot point for the actuator arm
- Works with the Pivot Plate Right for synchronized angle adjustment
- Uses M6 hardware with rubber or fiber washers for friction locking

<Tip>
Material: PETG, ABS, or ASA recommended for heat and creep resistance. PLA may deform under sustained load or elevated temperatures.
</Tip>
