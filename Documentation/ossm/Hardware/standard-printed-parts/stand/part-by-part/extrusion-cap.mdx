## Function

Cosmetic press-fit cap for exposed 3030 extrusion ends. This component:

- Covers sharp edges and prevents dust ingress
- Press-fits into the extrusion profile
- Used on non-floor-contact ends only (use Extrusion Feet for floor-contact ends)

<Tip>
Material: PLA or PETG (PETG recommended for higher temperature environments).
</Tip>
