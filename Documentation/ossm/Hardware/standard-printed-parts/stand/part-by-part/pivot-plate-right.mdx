## Function

One of two matching plates that enable angle adjustment on the OSSM stand. This component:

- Mounts to the 3030 aluminum extrusion frame
- Creates the right-side pivot point for the actuator arm
- Works with the Pivot Plate Left for synchronized angle adjustment
- Uses M6 hardware with rubber washers for friction-locked positioning
