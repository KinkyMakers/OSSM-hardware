## Function

The Dogbone Nuts are the nut-side of the PitClamp Mini quick‑release latch. Together with the Dogbone Bolts, they:

- Allow tool-free attachment and removal of the OSSM motor from a PitClamp Mini base
- Secure the clamp under load while maintaining consistent alignment
- Index rotation in 4° steps via a built‑in detent profile

## Print Settings

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Walls | 6 |
| Infill Type | Cross Hatch or Gyroid |
| Infill Percentage | 10% |
| Supports | Tree Supports, 5° threshold angle |

<Warning>
Avoid PLA for load‑bearing latch parts. PLA can creep and crack under clamp loads and elevated temperatures. Use PETG or ASA; ABS or Nylon for maximum heat and wear resistance.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Post-processing: Remove supports carefully from the detent pockets. Deburr with a light pass of a deburring tool or fine file — do not round over the detent edges, as crisp edges improve the 4° indexing feel.
</Tip>
