## Latching Mechanism

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 2 | M4x25 Socket Cap Head Bolt |
| 1 | M4x12 or M4x10 Socket Cap Head Bolt |
| 3 | M4 Hex Nut |

<Note>
If you use the print-in-place Upper+Handle variant (found in the Non-Standard folder), you can omit:
- 1x M4x12 or M4x10 Socket Cap Head Bolt
- 1x M4 Hex Nut
</Note>

## Mounting Rings

Select the ring that matches your motor.

<Tabs>
<Tab title="57AIM">

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 4 | M5x35 Socket Cap Head Bolt |
| 4 | M5x20mm Hex Coupling Nuts OR M5 Hex Nuts |

<Tip>
Using M5 Hex Coupling Nuts (20mm) enables the optional rear motor cover with interlocking ridges. This is the recommended setup.
</Tip>
