## Function

The PCB Box is the base of the OSSM electronics enclosure. It:

- Houses the OSSM PCB (ESP32 controller and motor driver interface)
- Routes cables through side openings for power and signal connections
- Pairs with the PCB Lid to fully enclose and protect the electronics
