This bracket allows you to mount standard gym weight plates to any aluminum extrusion, adding stability and reducing movement during operation.

## Compatibility

- **Weight plate size**: Standard 28-30mm inner diameter plates
- **Extrusion**: Compatible with any aluminum extrusion using M5 T-nuts

## Bill of materials

You need the following hardware for each bracket:

| Part | Quantity |
|------|----------|
| M5x12 Screw | 2 |
| M5 T-Nut | 2 |
